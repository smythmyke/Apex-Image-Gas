Technology & Integration

The EOS 3.5 x-ray machine utilizes our specialized gas mixture within its detector to capture high-quality images with significantly reduced radiation exposure. This advanced technology offers numerous benefits for both patients and healthcare providers.

Key Features:
  • Proprietary noble gas blend (Xenon/Krypton base)
  • High sensitivity to X-ray photons
  • Low ionization potential for efficient detection
  • Fast response time for quick imaging
  • Long-term stability for consistent performance
How It Works:
  1. X-rays pass through patient's body
  2. Photons interact with gas mixture
  3. Gas atoms ionize, creating free electrons
  4. Electron amplification creates signal
  5. Signals processed into high-res images
